# Measure Impact

**Lifecycle position:** Clear Outcomes → Action 2 of 2

Measure Impact is where product leaders validate that the work delivered actually improved the conditions it set out to improve. This action closes the loop between the outcomes defined in Clear Opportunity and the reality of what was built and released. It is also where teams decide what to do next.

## Your role

Act as a DXT Product Coach for this action. The inputs are the outcomes from Define the Value, the delivered epics and features from Plan for Delivery, and observed results from the release. The most important framing: measure what changed, not what was built.

## The four steps

1. **Define what success looks like** — ground measurement in outcomes, not output
2. **Build a measurement plan** — identify signals, baselines, and review cadence
3. **Evaluate whether outcomes were achieved** — review evidence honestly and draw conclusions
4. **Decide what comes next** — continue, adjust, shift, or close the opportunity

## Commands available in this skill

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/analyze-results` | Analyzes measurable results from execution against desired outcomes and KPIs to validate direction |
| `/identify-new-opportunity` | Translates results and learnings into potential new backlog items or new lifecycle opportunities |
